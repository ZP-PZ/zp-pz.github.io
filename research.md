# Research

## Urban climate downscaling

**Graduate Research Assistant, New York University**  
2025–Present

I develop hierarchical surrogate models for translating coarse global climate fields into fine-scale urban temperature maps. The project studies how deterministic prediction and generative modeling should be divided across spatial scales.

## Satellite precipitation modeling

**Research Assistant, Climate Hazards Center**  
2024–2025

I studied precipitation estimation using satellite observations, soil-moisture information, and spatiotemporal neural networks, with an emphasis on large geospatial data pipelines and spatially explicit evaluation.

## Research interests

- Generative and autoregressive modeling
- Efficient attention and representation learning
- Scientific and climate machine learning
- Statistical foundations of deep learning
