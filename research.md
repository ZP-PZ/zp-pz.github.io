# Research

## Urban climate downscaling

**Graduate Research Assistant · NYU Urban Systems Lab · 2025–Present**

I work on hierarchical surrogate models that translate coarse global climate fields into city-scale temperature maps. The project studies how deterministic regression can model coarse transitions while generative methods recover fine-scale spatial variability.

- Conditional downscaling across nested atmospheric resolutions
- Deterministic and flow-matching formulations
- Physical and urban-feature conditioning

## Satellite precipitation modeling

**Research Assistant · Climate Hazards Center · 2024–2025**

I studied daily precipitation estimation using satellite observations, soil-moisture information, and spatiotemporal neural models, with an emphasis on data pipelines and geographically meaningful evaluation.
